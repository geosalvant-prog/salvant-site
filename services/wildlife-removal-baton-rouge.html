Professional Wildlife Removal Services

Wildlife activity around homes and businesses can cause property damage and create safety concerns. Salvant Environmental Services provides wildlife removal services designed to safely remove nuisance animals and help prevent future problems.

Our technicians inspect properties, identify wildlife entry points, and implement removal methods appropriate for the type of animal present on the property.

Wildlife Trapping Services

In some situations, trapping may be required to safely remove animals from a property. Salvant Environmental Services provides trapping services designed to capture nuisance wildlife and remove them from residential or commercial environments.

Wildlife trapping services are used when animals have entered structures, attics, crawl spaces, or other areas where removal requires controlled capture methods.

Common Wildlife Problems

  • Raccoons in attics
  • Rodents entering crawl spaces
  • Squirrels nesting in roof structures
  • Opossums around residential properties
  • Wildlife damaging insulation or wiring

Wildlife Inspection And Prevention

Removing wildlife from a property is only one part of the solution. After removal, technicians may recommend preventative steps to help reduce the chances of future wildlife activity.

This may include identifying entry points, improving property conditions that attract wildlife, and helping property owners reduce conditions that allow animals to access structures.
