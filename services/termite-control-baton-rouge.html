Signs Of Termite Activity

Many termite infestations go unnoticed until significant damage has already occurred. Some of the most common warning signs include:

  • Mud tubes along foundations or walls
  • Soft or damaged wood
  • Discarded termite wings near windows
  • Swarming termites around lights
  • Wood structures that sound hollow when tapped
