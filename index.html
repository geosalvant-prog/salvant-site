Why Termite Control Matters In Louisiana

Louisiana's warm humid climate creates ideal conditions for termite colonies. Subterranean termites are one of the most common structural pests in Baton Rouge and can cause serious damage to homes and buildings if left untreated. Routine termite inspections and preventative treatment are essential to protecting property value.
