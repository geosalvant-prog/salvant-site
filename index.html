Why Termite Control Matters in Louisiana

Louisiana's warm climate creates ideal conditions for termite colonies. Subterranean termites are one of the most common structural pests in Baton Rouge and can cause significant damage to homes and commercial buildings. Preventative termite inspections and treatments are essential to protecting property value in the region.
